Our Team

Welcome to our site, Diversity United F.C., founded in 2016, Is proud to be one of the Saturday League team playing in Brighton and Worthing district league and Sussex F.A. affiliated Club.

Join Our Club

Home Ground

Located in the heart of Brighton, Preston Park is Diversity United own theatre of dreams. Complete with pavilion cafe it is perfect for a pre-match snack or half time coffee.

Home games kick off at 14:00 throughout the season.

Training venue

Stanley Deason is toward Brighton marina ,is Diversity United winter training venue. Our weekly winter training sessions take place up on the 3G astroturf from 18:00 - 20:00 on a Thursday evening

Each winter training session this year will collect donation to cover the increasing costs of league football in Brighton, if you want to join in or have any questions please contact Flavi.

Located near the University of Brighton Moulsecoomb campus, Wild Park is Diversity United summer training venue. Our weekly summer training sessions start from 17:30 - 19:30 on Tuesday and Thursday.

We have an open training policy for all new comers, if you want to join in or have any questions please contact Flavi.
